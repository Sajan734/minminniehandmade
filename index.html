Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We prioritize secure and reliable payment processing to ensure a seamless shopping experience for our customers.

How long does it take to receive my order?

If you are located within close proximity to our business, we offer the option for you to pick up your order directly from us. This can be a convenient and efficient way to receive your handmade goods. For orders that require shipping, the processing and shipping times may vary depending on the product, customization options, shipping destination, and any unforeseen delays. We aim to provide accurate and timely delivery estimates during the checkout process, and customers can also track their order status through our website.

Do you offer customization options?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our handmade products. We understand that our customers want to create a unique and personalized experience, so we offer a range of options such as personalized text, color choices, and design alterations. Simply select the customization option during the ordering process and provide us with the specific details you would like. Our skilled artisans will then craft your product with care and attention to detail, ensuring that you receive a truly one-of-a-kind item.

How can I contact you if I have any questions or concerns?

We are always happy to hear from our customers! If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about our handmade goods, you can reach us through several channels. You can use the contact form on our website, email us directly at minminnie.handmade@gmail.com, or message us on our social media platforms. Our customer service team is highly responsive and will get back to you as soon as possible. We value open communication and strive to provide a positive and personalized experience for all of our customers.
